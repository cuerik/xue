Background

Regulative vs. Mosaic development of C. elegans embryogenesis

People think C. elegans is mostly mosaic development

 

Question

Re-examine the role of induction in gut specification

 

Approach

Isolate early blastomeres and observe the differentiation of the isolates in the absence or presence of other blastomeres.

 

Control

The isolates were put back to test whether they were damaged during isolation

 

Results

1)     Gut often differentiated from the EMS isolate but not from other isolates

Conclusion: there is localized determinant that determine gut differentiation

 

2)     EMS needs to contact other cells early for gut to differentiate. Conclusion: induction from other cells are important for gut differentiation

 

3)     P2 but not other cells induce EMS to form gut

Conclusion: P2 is the signal-sending cell

 

Explanation of discrepancy with previous results.

Timing when blastomeres were isolated was not noted.

 

Future experiments

1)     Genetic screens
