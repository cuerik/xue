Essay 1

Goldstein B (1992). Induction of gut in Caenorhabditis elegans embryos. Nature 357:255-257 (Copies of this paper will be in the reserved section of Norlin Library)

I would like to see your comments on the following issues in your essay.

1.     What was the key question that this paper tried to address?

2.     What was the previous hypothesis or model?

3.     What was the basis of the previous hypothesis or model?

4.     What were the experimental approaches used by the author?

5.     What kind of experimental controls that the author did?

6.     What were the major experimental results obtained by the author?

7.     What was the major conclusion of this paper?

8.     What kind of future experiments this author or other scientists should do to further understand the key question raised in this paper?

9.     What do you learn from reading this paper?
