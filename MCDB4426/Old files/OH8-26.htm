Types of Embryo Development: Alberts et al. 3rd Ed.  p. 1069

Three different type of embryo development

 

a.      Mosaic Development (autonomous specification:  determined by local determinants and no cell-cell interaction)

     Experimental test:  remove one blastomere, the blastomere still develops as it will in the original embryo

 

b.     Regulative Development (conditional specification:  determined by cell-cell interaction and local determinants not important; e.g. mouse)

Experimental test:  if remove one blastomere, the other blastomere will take over and the embryo is fine.

 

c.     Intermediate (dependent on both local determinants and cell-cell interaction; Xenopus, fly and C. elegans)
