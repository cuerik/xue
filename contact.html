Xue Lab Job Announcements:

 

We are seeking highly motivated postdoctoral fellows, graduate students, and undergraduate students to join our team. If you are very interested in understanding basic biological processes such as cell death, lipid asymmetry and mitochondria biology, or seeking solutions to treat human disease such as chronic hepatitis, please contact us.

 

Please send your application to ding.xue@colorado.edu and include the following information:

      1. Your CV
      2. A list of your publications (for postdoc applicants)
      3. The research projects that you are interested in pursuing in the Xue laboratory
      4. A list of three references
